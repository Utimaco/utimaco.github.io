Hardware Security Modules for every industry

The protection of customer privacy, authenticity and authentication as well as data and communication integrity is increasingly recognized as the key enablers for the internet of things. To secure these, Hardware Security Modules (HSMs) are the first choice for providing the Root of Trust to all industries.

Industry convergence requires customizable HSM solutions

As the core tenants behind the idea known as the “Internet of Things”, interoperability and open exchange of data are the goals almost every industry is striving towards; from automotive to industrial IoT, energy to payment, government agencies to healthcare. At the same time, this open exchange of data requires trust in knowing where it came from, assurance that it is valid and confidence that it meets a certain level of integrity.

When sensitive information is shared, it needs to be protected. Securing the connectivity and data exchange between heterogeneous devices is imperative. The aim is to protect not only the data but also the privacy of the users, as well as to safeguard system infrastructure.

Utimaco addresses the following industries here below, amongst others.
